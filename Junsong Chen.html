About Me

I am currently a research intern at NVIDIA Research supervised by Dr. Enze Xie and Prof. Song Han. I am also a Ph.D. student at IIAU Lab of DLUT EECS, advised by Prof. Huchuan Lu. My research area is the intersection of AIGC and machine learning, especially algorithm and system co-design for deep learning and its applications.

I was also a Research Assistant at The University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Ping Luo.
